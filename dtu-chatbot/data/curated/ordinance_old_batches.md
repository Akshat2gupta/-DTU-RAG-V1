# DTU Old-Batch Ordinance Rules (2017-2019 batches) — differences from current rules

The current rules in the academic-rules document apply to AY 2020-21 batches
onwards. Batches admitted in 2017, 2018 and 2019 followed the older
ordinances, whose main differences are below. Grade letters and grade points
(O=10, A+=9, A=8, B+=7, B=6, C=5, P=4, F=0, plus I for Incomplete) and the
75% attendance requirement (Dean may relax up to 10%) are the same as today.

## 2017-18 batch — supplementary examinations instead of summer semester

Source: https://dtu.ac.in/Web/Academics/ordinance/B.Tech_Ordinance_2017-18.pdf
Batch-year: 2017

For students admitted before AY 2020-21 there was NO summer semester;
backlogs were cleared through Supplementary Examinations (ordinance R.25):

- A Supplementary Examination in a course is permissible only in the
  semester in which the course runs, held during vacations or within one
  month of the start of the next semester (announced by Dean Academic UG).
- The student carries forward the marks already obtained in the Mid-Term
  Examination, Practical Examination and Sessionals — only the End-Term
  component is re-examined, and marks are awarded as per performance in the
  supplementary exam.
- Make-up Examinations (for medical absence / 'I' grade conversion) were
  normally held along with the Supplementary Examination.
- The summer-semester system replaced supplementary examinations w.e.f. the
  AY 2020-21 batches.

## 2018 batch — supplementary examinations instead of summer semester

Source: https://dtu.ac.in/Web/Academics/ordinance/BTech_2018_ordinance.pdf
Batch-year: 2018

Same as the 2017-18 batch rules: backlogs were cleared through Supplementary
Examinations held during vacations or within one month of the next semester's
start, carrying forward Mid-Term/Practical/Sessional marks and re-examining
only the End-Term component. No summer semester existed for this batch
(introduced for AY 2020-21 batches onwards).

## 2019 batch — supplementary examinations instead of summer semester

Source: https://dtu.ac.in/Web/Academics/ordinance/BTech_2019_ordinance.pdf
Batch-year: 2019

Same as the 2017-18 batch rules: backlogs were cleared through Supplementary
Examinations (during vacations or within one month of the next semester's
start; Mid-Term/Practical/Sessional marks carry forward, only the End-Term is
re-examined). The summer semester replaced this system from the AY 2020-21
batches onwards.
