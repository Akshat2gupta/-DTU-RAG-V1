About Type-II Hostel

Type-II Hostels welcomes about 200 freshers every year and gets the honour to becomes their first accomodation. The Hostel is located near the Main gate of DTU to ensure hassle free welcome to the freshers

The hostel runs in Type-II flats in (2+2+1) system. The building is designed with energy-efficient systems and all efficient amenities to ensure the best living experience for our residents.

Hostel Philosophy

The Hostel is located near the main gate to ensure a comfortable stay for the residents. Being far away from all senior hostels it promises a ragging free environment for all the students.
