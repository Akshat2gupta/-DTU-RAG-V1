Hostels — Delhi Technological University

Delhi Technological University has 10 Boys and 3 Girls hostels in the campus that can accommodate around 2500 students. These hostels are:

Boys' Hostels

  1. Type — II Hostel
  2. Aryabhatt Hostel
  3. Bhaskaracharya Hostel
  4. Sir C. V. Raman Hostel
  5. Sir J.C. Bose Hostel
  6. Varahmihir Hostel
  7. Sir Vishveshwarya Hostel
  8. Homi Jehangir Bhabha Hostel
  9. Ramanujan Hostel
  10. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Hostel (AC)

Girls' Hostels

  1. Sister Nivediata Hostel
  2. Kalpana Chawla Hostel
  3. Virangana Lakshmibai Hostel, Block-1 (AC)
  4. Virangana Lakshmibai Hostel, Block-2 (AC)

Mess Services: The mess services are provided in six of the hostels. These are Sir Visvesvaraya Hostel, Sir C. V. Raman Hostel, AryaBhatta Hostel, Homi Jahangir Bhabha Hostel, Sir J.C. Bose Hostel and Sister Nivedita. These cater to the requirements of all the students residing within the campus.


Common Facilities: Every hostel has its own common room and gymnasium. Additionally every hostel subscribes to the latest magazines and newspapers for the residents.
